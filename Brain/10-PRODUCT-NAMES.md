# 10 — Product Name Brainstorm

This was the brainstorm behind the chosen name, **`HexaCore`**. Kept for the record.
Brief: **catchy, fancy, "gray" — the in-between space gray-hat operators live in** (neither
pure white-hat nor black-hat). Bonus points for fitting the **brain + heart** motif and your
**Sahasrakshi** ("thousand-eyed") heritage.

> Quick caveats before you fall in love with one: (1) check the `.com`/`.io` domain and a basic
> trademark search; (2) **avoid names already taken by offensive-security tooling** — e.g.
> *Cobalt* (Cobalt Strike / Cobalt.io), *Sliver*, *Havoc*, *Mythic*, *Merlin*, *Empire* are C2
> frameworks; *Nessus/Nuclei/ZAP* are scanners; *Aegis/Sentinel/Guardian* are overused in blue-team
> land. I've kept the list clear of the obvious collisions but still verify.

## Shortlist to say out loud (the "does it sound like a product" test)

Try each in the sentence *"Scanned by \_\_\_\_."* and *"Powered by the \_\_\_\_ platform."*

1. **Penumbra** — "Scanned by Penumbra." ✅ premium, distinctive.
2. **Janus** — "Powered by the Janus platform." ✅ sharp, dual-meaning.
3. **Kestrel** — "Scanned by Kestrel." ✅ clean, hunter energy.
4. **Argus** — "Scanned by Argus." ✅ all-seeing, ties to your brand.
5. **Trinetra** — "Powered by Trinetra." ✅ heritage, all-seeing, unique.
6. **Graymatter** — "Scanned by Graymatter." ✅ friendly, on-theme.
7. **Chhaya** — "Powered by Chhaya." ✅ elegant, distinctive.

## How it was locked in

1. Name chosen: **HexaCore** (verify domain + a quick trademark/collision check before launch).
2. `HexaCore` is used across `Brain/**`, `.antigravity/knowledge/**`, and repo scaffolding.
   (If it ever changes, global find-replace `HexaCore` → the new name.)
3. Keep `Brain/` and `Heart/` folder names as-is — they're the anatomy, not the brand.
4. Tell Antigravity the chosen name in the first build prompt so it names packages/UI consistently.

My personal top three, if you want a nudge: **Penumbra** (best concept), **Argus** or **Trinetra**
(best "thousand-eyed" tie to Sahasrakshi), **Janus** (best nod to the gated, dual-nature design).
